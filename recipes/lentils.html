Smash Lentil QUESO-TACOS! (VEGAN)

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup DRIED Lentils, washed, rinsed and drained. (green, brown or black lentils work)
  • 1 can plum tomatoes (Or any can of tomatoes)
  • 2 1/2 Cups water
  • 1 Onion, diced.
  • 2 Shallots, diced.
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced.
  • 3-4 TBS Olive Oil
  • Handful tortilla chips, crushed.
  • 1 Â½ Cups Shredded Smoked Vegan Cheese, (Optional)
  • 1 Pack small to medium sized tortilla wraps or soft taco shells
  • 1 Head Lettuce, shredded.
  • 2 TBS Chili Powder
  • 2 TBS Cumin
  • 1 tsp Onion, granulated.
  • 1 tsp Salt, kosher
  • 1 tsp Black pepper

Directions

  1. Add diced onion, shallots, and garlic to a bowl
  2. Add Olive Oil to medium pan
  3. Toast spices in Olive Oil
  4. Sautee ingredients from step 1
  5. Add cleaned/dried lentils
  6. Add plum tomatoes
  7. Add 3 cups of water (TOTAL INCLUDING TOMATOES)
  8. Bring to boil, then simmer (STIR OCCASIONALLY)
  9. Remove from heat for 15 minutes and add crushed tortilla chips
  10. Cool in fridge for 2-3 hours
  11. Make Pico (See linked Sub-Recipe)
  12. Once cooled, add cheese to lentils
  13. Take 1 tortilla, add lentil filling to half and folder over
  14. Lightly oil and salt pan (To taste)
  15. Flip
  16. Repeat 13-15 as many times as needed
